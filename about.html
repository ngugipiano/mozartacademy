ABOUT MOZART ACADEMY OF MUSIC

Director's message

Welcome

Mr.Sam Warigah.
Director at Mozart Academy Of Music.

"Our aim is to popularize classical music by providing music lessons at affordable costs so that we can have as many people as possible to benefit from learning classical music.

Some of the benefits of learning how to play classical music include:
  • Decreases blood pressure. According to a study by Oxford University, classical music helps to reduce blood pressure.
  • Boosts Memory - The study also showed that people who listen to Mozart’s music have better memory.
  • Sparks creativity - You develop a more creative mindset.
  • Reduce stress levels - A study found that classical music reduces stress.
  • Supercharges brain power.
  • Fights depression.
  • Relieves pain - According to researchers in London, patients listening to classical music used significantly less pain medication.
  • Makes you happy/improves mood. A 2013 study found that music can help people to have a better mood.
  • Improves productivity - A study performed by researchers at the University of Maryland found that classical music can help improve radiologist’s efficiency and accuracy.

It is therefore our hope that many pupils, students, and teachers in conservatories will benefit from working together with us."

Charter

We offer music lessons in a wide range of instruments and music theory with a focus on classical music at friendly costs. Our main aim is to popularize classical music.

To have the best music school in Africa and beyond that offers the best quality of music education in classical music and to develop a resource center for studying Mozart’s music including analysis of his autographs ,life ,history and offering master classes and performance opportunities to both teachers and students in relation to his music and other related works.

  • 1. It is reasonable to assume that most errors which students make in music are due to inattention on the part of the teacher than to earn eagerness to see the pupil progress rapidly. Sometimes they may be the fruit of wrong material.
  • 2. We have endeavored to solve this problem by presenting the teaching material in a step by step manner taking every interest in the pedagogical development of the student with respect to technical dexterity, sight reading, memorization and aural skills .The students also benefit from the following areas:
    • Participating in the school orchestra team to develop and enhance their playing.
    • Participating in recitals organized by the school and in partnership with other organizations.
    • Exchange programs and master classes help the students to develop a better understanding of their instrument.
  • 3. Most of our students get very good grades in ABRSM ( Associated Board of the Royal Schools of Music) exams with a majority scoring distinction in all grade levels due to the quality of education they receive from our institution.
  • 4. We also offer ABRSM diploma courses in a variety of the instruments.
