Bach Invention 7 (BVW 778)

Bach's Invention 7 (BWV 778) is a masterful keyboard work showcasing contrapuntal brilliance. Its intricate intertwining of two voices demonstrates the genius of the Baroque era, highlighting Bach's compositional prowess.
